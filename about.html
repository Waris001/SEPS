About Our School

"Learning Today, Leading Tomorrow."

Welcome to Star Educational Public School

Star Educational Public School is a trusted educational institution located in Dhanohari, Post Shahpur (Dumariaganj), District Siddharth Nagar, Uttar Pradesh. Recognized by the Government, our school is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, strong moral values, and the overall development of every child.

We offer education from Lower Kindergarten (LKG) to Class 10, following the NCERT curriculum with English as the primary medium of instruction. Our experienced and dedicated teachers create a supportive learning environment where every student is encouraged to think independently, build confidence, and achieve their full potential.

We are committed to providing a safe, disciplined, and student-friendly campus equipped with modern teaching practices that inspire curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong passion for learning.

Our campus offers a wide range of facilities, including:

  • ✔ Qualified and experienced teaching staff
  • ✔ NCERT-based curriculum with English medium education
  • ✔ CCTV-enabled campus ensuring student safety
  • ✔ Spacious classrooms with comfortable seating arrangements
  • ✔ School transportation facility
  • ✔ Special focus on English, Hindi, Urdu, Science, Mathematics, and Computer Education
  • ✔ Personalized academic support for students requiring additional guidance
  • ✔ Clean drinking water and hygienic sanitation facilities
  • ✔ A caring, inclusive, and child-friendly learning environment

Our Mission
